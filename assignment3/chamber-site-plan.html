Site Name: Tri-Cities Chambers

Why: This name reflects is location.

Site Purpose:

The Tri-Cities Chambers seeks to broaden the sights of the locals. By creating this website, we hope to better reach the citizens. This will both help improve local businesses, but also provide value where they might not have know was available.

Scenarios

1. Tourists wondering where to go.

2. Customers living locally who are wondering what is going on/what they can do.

3. Business owners looking for publicity or become a member of the chamber.

Color Schema

Main Color: FB8500

I will be using this color because of orange's association with warmth and excitement, leading locals to get excited about local businesses. This will be used in headings primarily.

Accent: 8ECAE6

I will be using this color because of blue's association with dependability and wisdom. We want the locals to trust us as we serve them and their community. This will be used in supporting sections and as a selection color in menus.
